Why Dark Mode?

Many people prefer a dark background in low light or at night. It can reduce eye strain and save energy on OLED screens. Here are few benifits:

Reduced eye strain

Softer contrast in dim environments can make long reading sessions more comfortable.

Better for OLED

True black pixels stay off, which can extend battery life on phones and laptops.
